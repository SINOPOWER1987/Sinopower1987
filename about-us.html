About Company

SINO POWER . is a licensed International freight forwarder established since 1987 and amongst the first batch of operators being awarded the NVOCC license by the China Ministry of Transportation. We are uniquely positioned to handle all aspects of your shipment from raw material to end user. Our full array of ocean related services include Ocean forwarding, NVOCC consolidation as well as custom-made supply chain management services.

Our experienced professionals are trained to guide you through the complexities of global shipping and are committed to providing 'YOU' our valuable customer with the most personable services in the ever-competitive shipping industry.

Headquartered in Hong Kong, we have successfully established wholly owned branch offices in Shanghai, Tianjin, Xian and Qingdao over the years. To further enhance our services, we work with selected agents in Dalian, Guangzhou, Nanjing, Ningbo, Shenzhen, Xiamen, Taiwan and Hong Kong. Our overseas partners and agents in North America, Middle East, Europe and Asia complement our extensive networks in the PRC.

Our goal is to reduce your burden from all shipping and logistics related issues to allow you to focus and grow your core business as we believe that we are a natural extension to your business. By selecting Oceanlink as your preferred forwarder, you will get to enjoy the expertise and leveraged pricing with many leading carriers of the World without contractual liability.

Our commitment to focus on Total Solution and Total Customer Satisfaction (TCS) is our top priority and we take pride in delivering world-class services to you because we believe in growing with your business.
