Ocean Freight

Through long-term partnership with core carriers EMC, YML,OOCL,COSCO, HPL, CMA, MSK, etc, not only we can offer competitive rates, but also quality service to our clients by a team of experienced staff and area experts.

Air Freight

Sinopower Transportation is a leader in global air freight services and has been recognized as a top International Air Transport Association (IATA) forwarder for over 30 years.

Sinopower's global airline partnerships are awarded based on the specific price, transit time, fixed allocations and lift capacity of carriers in each major trade lane and their ability to add efficiency to our clients' supply chain management.

Logistics

Sinopower continues to modernize its warehouse and distribution facilities to better serve its clients in terms of warehouse management, inventory control, order fulfillment and logistics spending. Sinopower can provide the solution to any shipping requirement and serve as a single-source logistics window for all product or components moving through your supply chain. Our services include warehousing & storage, project cargo, sorting & repackaging, labeling and palletizing.

Domestic Cargo

Sinopower Transportation’s strategy is to leverage our global network, systems and established list of services so that we can provide a seamless movement of cargo to our customers within domestic areas of their supply chain.

Our network gives us the flexibility to track, deliver and reduce cost. This enables Sinopower to monitor goods cradle to grave, door to door, or to airport only.

Customs Brokerage

Our team of worldwide professionals expedites the customs clearance process using state of the art software and electronic interfaces with each government agency. Our capabilities include pre-clearance of both air and ocean shipments, regular inbound services from all major international origins, ABI entry processing, cartage and delivery services, duty drawback assistance, classification database services, compliance reviews and remote filing.
